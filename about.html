About Us

The Story of Venus Furnitures

Venus Furnitures is a trusted furniture showroom in Pune and an authorised dealer of Godrej Interio (division of Godrej & Boyce).

We are an ISO 9001:2008 certified company offering wooden, steel and modular furniture along with interior solutions for homes, offices, farms, resorts, hospitals, educational institutes and corporate offices.

Our 4,000+ sq. ft showroom at Market Yard, Pune showcases sofa sets, armchairs, dining tables, wall units, bedroom sets, office furniture, décor accessories and custom furniture, so you can see and feel the quality before you decide.

Beyond products, we also undertake turnkey projects – from planning and design to execution – giving you a single partner for furniture and interiors.
